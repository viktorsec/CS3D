### Usage

Play the game by entering following commands

- `GO [NORTH | EAST | SOUTH | WEST | AROUND]`
- `SHOOT [NORTH | EAST | SOUTH | WEST | AROUND]`
- `LOOK [NORTH | EAST | SOUTH | WEST | AROUND]`
- `HELP`
- `DIG`
- `MOMO`
- `STATS`
- `MAP`
- `QUIT`
